<p style="font-size: 31px;">&nbsp;What is reposting?</p>
<p>Reposting describes the uncredited re-uploading of (mostly copyrighted) files without the permission of the author. Reposting is one of the worst things a mod author can go through, and here is why:</p>
<table style="width: 100%;">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td style="width: 33%;">
<p style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/466/747/stop_icon_0.png" />Malicious software</p>
<p>If a mod gets reposted, the mod author doesn't have<br />any control over the files on the reposting site. This means that malware/adware could get on your computer when downloading/using the mod.</p>
</td>
<td style="width: 33%;">
<p style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/466/748/stop_icon_1.png" width="36" height="36" />No income for Developers</p>
<p>Developers live from income. If you download from reposting sites, they won't get any revenue, which could mean less mods from them in the future.</p>
</td>
<td style="width: 33%;">
<p style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/466/749/stop_icon_2.png" width="36" height="36" />Outdated versions</p>
<p>Reposting sites might link you to outdated versions, which could mean more bugs, crashes and errors for you.</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<p style="font-size: 31px;">Get Involved</p>
<p>What can you do to stop mod reposting?</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold; color: #c11f20;">1. Don't visit reposting sites</span> <br />These sites mostly make ad revenue from every visit. Visiting reposting sites supports them. You can download our free extension for Chrome and Firefox <a href="https://stopmodreposts.org/extension">here</a></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold; color: #c11f20;">2. Report reposting sites</span> <br /> If you discover a new site and don't see it in our lists, please notify us about it via the <a href="https://stopmodreposts.org/submit.html">Submit Form</a> or our <a href="https://discord.gg/tZBeUy3">Discord Server</a>.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold; color: #c11f20;">3. Support the movement</span> <br /> Every follower, like and retweet helps spread awareness amongst users who download mods. You can also use our hashtag <a href="https://twitter.com/StopModReposts">#StopModReposts</a> or you can also donate on OpenCollective.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold; color: #c11f20;">4. For mod authors: Request the removal of your mod</span> <br /> You can file a DMCA takedown notice to request the removal of your mod. Please keep in mind: DMCAs are legal documents, and you must make sure you understand the full procedure, and the consequenses for not following it.</p>
